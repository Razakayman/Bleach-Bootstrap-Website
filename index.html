Bleach

Shonen Jump Series

Bleach is a successful Shonen Jump manga that ran for over 10 years and had more than 70 volumes. The series has in-total, sold over 100 million volumes.

Ways to view

Bleach has many different mediums for people to enjoy it. Here are a few of the major ways to view.

Manga

Movies

Tite Kubo

Tite Kubo is the author behind the manga Bleach. He wrote his first manga, Zombiepowder in 1999.

Kubo submitted the original story concept to Shonen Jump but was rejected. Kubo then recieved a letter of support from Akira Toriyama, creator of Dragonball.

Bleach was published in Shone Jump in 2001 to 2016 and ran for 686 chapters.

Other Characters

Bleach has a wide range of characters, including fan favorites such as Renji, Hitsugaya, Orihime, Grimmjow, and more.

Kubo said in an interview once that he felt empty when Bleach had ended as he had become attatched to all the different characters he created.

A common criticism Bleach recieves is its lack of character development for the characters. While there are many characters in total, few actually developed as the story continued.
